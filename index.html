The problem

Stumps don't just disappear.

Left alone, stumps attract beetles, carpenter ants, and rot. They're tripping hazards, they block mowing, and they pull nutrients from anything you try to grow nearby. The longer they sit, the worse it gets.

The solution

We grind it below the surface.

We grind your stump down below ground level — no chemicals, no heavy digging, no mess left behind. Most jobs are done in under an hour. Fill it, seed it, and forget it was ever there.

FAQ

Common questions.

How do you measure the diameter? +

We measure at the widest point of the stump at ground level. If you're estimating before we arrive, running a tape measure across the top of the stump is close enough to get an accurate quote.

How long does the job take? +

Most stumps are ground out in 30–60 minutes. Very large stumps or several stumps in one visit may take a bit longer, but we'll give you an honest time estimate when we schedule.

What happens to the wood chips? +

Grinding produces wood chips and mulch that fill the hole left behind. You can rake it level, mix it into the soil, or we can haul it away — just let us know when you book.

Do you handle multiple stumps? +

Yes. Each stump is priced individually using the same formula — $100 minimum or $3 per inch of diameter, whichever is greater. Just add a note in the quote form with how many you have.

How far in advance do I need to book? +

Same-week availability is typical. Submit a quote request and we'll follow up within 24 hours to confirm the price and find a time that works for you.
